Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know before getting started.

What is a WhatsApp bot for a clinic or salon?

It is an automated assistant on your business WhatsApp number that replies to common customer questions — timings, prices, address — and books appointments 24×7, so your staff does not have to answer every message manually.

Do customers need to install anything?

No. Customers just message your normal WhatsApp number and the bot replies instantly. There is nothing to install or download.

Is it safe and allowed by WhatsApp?

Yes. It runs on the official WhatsApp Cloud API from Meta — not an unofficial hack — so your number stays safe. It also collects only name, service and time, never medical or sensitive data, keeping it DPDP-compliant.

How much does it cost?

Plans start at ₹299/month for a single number, with Pro at ₹799/month and Business at ₹1,999/month. A 7-day free trial is available.

Which businesses is it for?

Clinics, dental and skin practices, salons, spas, coaching and tuition centres — any local business that takes appointments or bookings over WhatsApp.
