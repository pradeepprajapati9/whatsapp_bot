Privacy Policy

FrontDesk Bot · Last updated: July 2026

FrontDesk Bot ("we", "our", "the service") provides an automated WhatsApp assistant that helps businesses such as clinics, salons and coaching centres answer common customer questions and take appointment requests. This policy explains what information we handle and how.

Information we collect

When a customer messages a business that uses FrontDesk Bot, we process only the information needed to answer the message and record an appointment request:

  • The customer's name (only if they provide it to book an appointment)
  • The customer's WhatsApp phone number
  • The chosen service and the customer's preferred day/time
  • The text of the messages sent to the business number

We do not ask for, and the bot is designed not to collect, sensitive personal information.

How we use it

  • To reply to the customer's questions automatically
  • To record appointment/booking requests for the business to follow up
  • To notify the business owner of new requests

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Message data is shared only with the specific business the customer contacted, and with WhatsApp/Meta as required to deliver messages through the official WhatsApp Business Platform.

Data retention

Appointment and message records are kept only as long as needed for the business to serve the customer, and are deleted on request.

Your choices

Customers can ask the business to access or delete their information at any time. A customer can also stop messaging the bot at any point.
